Refund & Cancellation Policy

Subscription Services (Monthly/Yearly)

  • You can cancel your subscription at any time. Your access to the paid features will continue until the end of the current billing period.
  • Annual Subscriptions: If you cancel an annual subscription within 14 days of the initial purchase, you are eligible for a full refund. No refunds will be issued for cancellations made after 14 days.
  • Monthly Subscriptions: Monthly subscription fees are non-refundable. When you cancel, you will not be charged for the subsequent months.

Pay-As-You-Go / Credit Packs

  • Credits are deducted from your balance as you use the services.
  • Unused credits are non-refundable and typically do not expire unless otherwise stated at the time of purchase.

One-Time Service Fees (e.g., Custom Model Training)

  • If you cancel the service request before our team has commenced work, you are eligible for a full refund, minus any transaction fees.
  • No refunds are available once work has begun on a custom project, as resources will have been allocated.

Service Downtime or Failures

  • If our services are unavailable for an extended period due to internal technical failures (not including scheduled maintenance), we may, at our discretion, issue a pro-rata refund or service credit for the affected period to our active subscribers.

Refund Processing

  • Refunds processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

Contact for Cancellations & Refunds

  • To cancel a service or request a refund, please contact our support team with your account details and the reason for your request.
    • Email: support@coxist.ai
In case of any refunds approved by Coxist AI, it will take 5-7 business days for the refund to be credited to the original payment method.
